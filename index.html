My Philosphy

I take a user-centered approach to full stack engineering and design, solving real problems by prioritizing usability, accessibility, and performance. My goal is to create seamless experiences that meet users' needs at every touchpoint, whether through front-end components or backend logic.

I focus on simplicity, reusable components, and best practices to reduce redundancy and foster innovation. My work not only serves end-users but also empowers teams to iterate and build confidently. I collaborate across teams to design systems that bring long-term value through continuous iteration based on research and user feedback.
